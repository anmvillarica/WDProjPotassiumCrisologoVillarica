MEET THE CREATORS
Hello! I am Tala!
I'm the main artist for our website.

I designed the webpages, two of our sprites, and the backgrounds for our game. I enjoy making art and designing with HTML. I’ve been getting into older Pokemon games (I’ve been trying to finish Pokemon: Gold and Silver), and I’ve been meaning to try pixel art for a while. That was my inspiration for the aesthetic of our game.

It was very cool to make a legitimate website with someone else, and I am very grateful to Arianna for being the best partner. It’s a very cute project all in all, and I am proud of it.
