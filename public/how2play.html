MECHANICS
This website teaches the syntax for very simple HTML and JS codes. In every level, there is a sprite that needs to be moved in a specific way to complete the level.

The instructions will be shown at the top of your screen.

At the bottom of the screen, there will be a progress bar that shows how close you are to winning. Complete all three missing pieces to win!



Simply complete the code by entering the needed information into the blanks on the box in each level. You can choose to restart the level by clicking the replay icon at the bottom of each box. Click the speaker icon at the left hand side of every level to play music!

Have fun and enjoy playing our game!
